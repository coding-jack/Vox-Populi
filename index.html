About Vox Populi

Founded in 1997, Vox Populi Industry Specific Translations Company™ is a full-service language agency offering translation, interpretation, and language training services. Our Translations are accurate, using words to fit within the context of the target language while still retaining your original message, with high quality accuracy, punctuality, and confidentiality. If you are unavailable to meet face-to-face, choose phone interpretation. Our phone interpreters use the consecutive interpretation which means that there is a pause between the conversations for the interpreter to render the dialogue into the target language.

Cultural competency is useful for people and business and it is a quick useful understanding of the culture, and the way for a clear communication. We provide language classes for those interested in traveling overseas who want to be able to confidently communicate with native speakers and have meaningful dialogues. Our comprehensive language classes use important tools such as audio, video, textbooks, and recorded conversation, to practice and improve reading, pronunciation, listening, and speaking.

Our interpreters have excellent listening skills and trained memory, storing all information spoken and quickly conveying into the target language.
